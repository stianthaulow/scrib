Scrib is a lightweight text workspace for Android. Paste or share text in, use the editing helpers to isolate exactly what you need, then copy it back out.

Works well for things like extracting a phone number from a wall of text: share to Scrib, tap the number, copy.

Features:

- Share target: any app can share text directly to Scrib via Android's share sheet
- Smart cleaning: strips trailing URLs and surrounding quotation marks automatically on share
- Cursor controls: move left, right, up, down and select the word under the caret — no fiddling with touch handles
- Clipboard actions: append, replace from clipboard, or copy the note or current selection in one tap
- Persistent undo: undo/redo history saved across restarts
