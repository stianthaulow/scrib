Scrib - Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-04-27

Overview

Scrib is a local-only text workspace. It does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything you write stays on your device.

Data collection

Scrib does not collect any data. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected or stored outside your device.
  • No analytics, crash reporting, or usage tracking of any kind.
  • No advertising SDKs or third-party tracking libraries.
  • No user accounts or authentication.

Data storage

Notes are saved locally on your device using Android's standard file storage. No data is uploaded to any server. No data leaves your device.

Third-party services

Scrib does not integrate with any third-party services. The app has no network permissions and makes no network requests.

Data sharing

No data is shared with any third party because no data is collected.

Your rights

Since no data is collected or stored outside your device, there is nothing to request access to, correct, or delete on our end. To delete your data, uninstall the app or clear its storage in Android Settings.

Children's privacy

Scrib does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at this URL with a new effective date.
